Boats, Booze, and Betrayal

Boats, Booze, and Betrayal is a board game that myself and 4 other students originally created for our Game Design and Development I course.

The game gives control of pirate ships over to 4 players. On their turn, players can encounter several events, collect booze and/or gold, and fight other players for their possessions.

Development of the game has continued beyond the class, and we showcased the game at Roc Game Fest in October 2019. I helped design the game's mechanics and write the rulebook. I also created the trailer found here.
