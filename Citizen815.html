Citizen 815

Citizen 815 is a 2D platformer that I'm creating with Cori Mori as part of the MAGIC Maker Grant Program.

The game has two primary mechanics. The first mechanic is a bow and arrow which launches the player in the opposite direction of their aim. The second mechanic is an altered state of mind which enchances the player's movement and combat abilities.

We've taken an extreme focus on player movement and game feel, spending the majority of our 4 months in the Maker Program creating the best moment-to-moment player interactions we possibly can.

As part of the Maker Program, I've had the unique oppotunity to formally pitch, manage, and develop this personal project in a professional environment. The project and grant program has been highly influencial for me; teaching me valuable lessons about project managment, scope, and direction.
