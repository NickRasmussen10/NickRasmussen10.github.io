School is Canceled

School is Canceled is a solo project I developed in 72 hours for Ludum Dare 46.

The game is a 2D platformer about a handful of whiteboard drawings coming to life and caring for their creator, Josh, as best as they can.

Having recently taught myself Unity's animation system during Global Game Jam, I wanted to dedicate my time in Ludum Dare to refining my animation workflow. As a result, I focused heavily on polish in this game.

I took on a particularly fun challenge with the art style. All of the characters are my family's real-world whiteboard drawings that I photographed, isolated, rigged, and animated into digital characters using Photoshop and Unity.

Overall, I'm quite proud of the level of polish I managed to add on the tight deadline, which I attibute to keeping to a strict set of core features to avoid overscoping.
